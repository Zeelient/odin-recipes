Bologne Cake

An unconventional dish that may cause you to gag at the first sight, but isn't as gross as you think it would be
This simple recipe is a beloved staple in the South and only needs 3 ingredients, it's perfect as a centerpiece at parties,
as a 5-minute meal, or if you occasionally don't feel like watching your health. Either way, grab some crackers, a knife,
cut yourself a slice and dig in.


  • 1 pound of sliced bologne
  • 1 8oz packet of cream cheese or a tub of mayo
  • mustard

Steps

  1. Take the bologne and stack the slices together to form a tower
  2. Smother the tower in cream cheese or mayo
  3. Decorate with mustard
