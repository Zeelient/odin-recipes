Pickled Pigs Feet


This popular pickled snack hailing from Eastern Europe is now a popular treat in the South
In America. Tasty, simple, and does not take too long to make. Plus, it has a long shelf life

  • pigs feet
  • 2 quarts of vinegar
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of grated horseradish
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon of whole allspice
  • 1 by leaf

Steps

  1. Thouroughly scald, scrape, and clean pigs feet
  2. Lightly sprinkled with salt and leave them in fridge
    for 4 to 8 hours.
  3. Afterwards, wash feet in clean, cold water
  4. Place in hot water and cook until meat can be
    from the bones
  5. Mix remaining ingredients and bring to a boil
  6. Pack the feet into hot jars while leaving 1/2-inch
    headspace
  7. Fill jars 1/2-inch from top with vinegar solution
  8. Leave feet in jars and enjoy whenever you want
