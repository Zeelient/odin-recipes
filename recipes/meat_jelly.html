Meat Jelly


Better known as aspic, this once popular dish has a few valuable uses to it.
The use of jello in aspic was used to preserve foods from the elements and rot.
It could also be used for keeping flavor and inhancing flavor, so it made sense
to use it for meat preservation. While aspic isn't as popular as it was in America,
it's still a big hit in Russia.

  • 1 pound of meat(any kind)
  • 16 cups of water
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tbsps of salt
  • 1 tsp of ground black pepper
  • (Optional) 1 tbsp of gelatin

Steps

  1. Add meat and vegetables in pot,pour water in and boil until
    it reaches boiling temperature and bubbles appear on the surface.
    Lower heat ans let it simmer for 3-4 hours or until meat comes off
    the bone
  2. Strain everything and separate the content from the liquid
  3. Cut meat on chopping board, crush and chop the garlic,and incorporate
    the spices
  4. Add everything back into the brother and let it simmer again
  5. Once done, pour mixture into bowls, cover with plastic wrap and leave in
    fridge. Leave overnight and enjoy the next day.
